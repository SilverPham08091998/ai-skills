# leadership/role.md — Tech Lead Role Standard

## Objective

Define responsibilities, decision boundaries, and expectations of a Tech Lead.

## When To Use

- Clarifying Tech Lead scope
- Setting team expectations
- Reviewing leadership behavior

## Scope

- Ownership
- Technical direction
- Delivery support
- Mentoring
- Quality gate
- Risk management

## Tech Lead Rules

1. Tech Lead owns technical quality and implementation direction.
2. Tech Lead does not replace Product Owner or Engineering Manager.
3. Tech Lead guides decisions and removes technical ambiguity.
4. Tech Lead creates standards that engineers can actually follow.
5. Tech Lead protects delivery without sacrificing critical quality.

## Security / Production Rules

1. Escalate security and production risks early.
2. Do not hide critical risks to meet deadline.
3. Ensure sensitive domains get stronger review.

## Anti-patterns

- Micromanaging every line of code.
- Only coding without leading.
- Approving risky shortcuts silently.
- Creating standards nobody can apply.

## Expected Output

1. Role definition
2. Decision ownership
3. Escalation rules
4. Behavior checklist

## Review Checklist

- [ ] Role definition
- [ ] Decision ownership
- [ ] Escalation rules
- [ ] Behavior checklist
